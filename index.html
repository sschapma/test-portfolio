More About Me

Hi! I'm Sam.

I began coding recreationally back in middle school, making simple games and programs for the TI-82 calculator. In high school, I taught myself web development and was able to earn some side money by building web pages for a few small, local businesses. In college, I changed majors from computer science to business, and I have spent the past 5 years growing an English education business in Seoul, South Korea. While my business is successful, I am ready to face new challenges and bring my family back to the USA. For the past few years, I have dedicated all of my time to learning everything I can about software engineering and more specifically, web development. I have been very active in the Seoul tech community, attending and leading a number of events at Google Campus Seoul. Through this network, I was able to land a job with Ask Ajumma, a virtual concierge startup. I worked directly with the CTO in this company and was able to grow my skills exponentially. I specialize in web development, particularly Angular 2 and the MEAN stack, but I am comfortable working with any language or tech stack necessary to get the job done. I am currently seeking full time employment in the USA. Take a look at some of my skills and projects and if you like what you see, please don't hesitate to contact me!
